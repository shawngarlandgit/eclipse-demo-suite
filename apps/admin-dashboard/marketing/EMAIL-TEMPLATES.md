## Notes for Sales Team

### Best Practices

1. **Personalize the first line** — Reference something specific about their dispensary (Weedmaps reviews, Instagram post, store location, etc.)

2. **Keep it short** — These templates are already tight. Don't add fluff.

3. **Subject lines matter** — A/B test different subjects with the same body copy.

4. **Follow up consistently** — Day 1 → Day 5 → Day 10 → Move to monthly nurture.

5. **Always offer an out** — "If not, no worries" reduces pressure and paradoxically increases responses.

### Response Handling

- **"What does it cost?"** — Don't quote over email. Say: "Depends on your setup — easiest to show you on a quick call and I'll give you exact numbers."

- **"We're locked into a contract"** — "Totally get it. When does that end? I'll set a reminder to check back."

- **"We're happy with [competitor]"** — "Good to hear! Mind if I ask what you like most about them? Always curious what's working."

- **"Not the right time"** — "Understood. Would it be okay to check back in 3 months?"
