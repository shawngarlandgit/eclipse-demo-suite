Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about getting started.

Pricing & Contracts
No long-term contracts. After your 90-day pilot, you can continue month-to-month or commit to an annual plan for a 15% discount. You can cancel with 30 days notice.
Implementation fees are split into milestone payments tied to deliverables, so you only pay as value is delivered. If we don't hit a milestone, you don't pay for it.
Integrations & Tech
No. We integrate with your existing POS (Flowhub, Dutchie, Treez, etc.) rather than replacing it. We sit on top and unify data across systems.
We currently support Flowhub, Dutchie, Treez, Cova, and Blaze. If you use a different POS, let us know - we can usually add support within 2-4 weeks.
Compliance & Security
We integrate directly with METRC via API to monitor compliance, generate reports, and alert you to potential issues before they become violations.
You do. Your data is yours, always. We never sell or share your data. You can export everything at any time, and we delete it within 30 days if you leave.
Onboarding & Support
Minimal. We handle 90% of the setup. You'll need about 4-6 hours total from your team during the first two weeks for training and feedback sessions.
All tiers include email and chat support with same-day response. Specialist and above get a dedicated success manager and priority phone support.
