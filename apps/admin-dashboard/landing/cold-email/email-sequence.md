## Voice Guidelines

**Do:**
- Sound like a person, not a company
- Mention Maine constantly
- Be direct about what you want (a call)
- Show you've done this before (government software)
- Create urgency without being sleazy

**Don't:**
- Use corporate buzzwords
- Mention "AI" too much (sounds like hype)
- Apologize for following up
- Sound desperate
- Over-promise
